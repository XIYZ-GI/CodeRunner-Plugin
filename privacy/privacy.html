Privacy Policy for Code Runner

This Privacy Policy describes how Code Runner (the “Plugin”) collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use the Plugin.

By using the Plugin, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

The Plugin does not collect any personal information or any other thing from you. The Plugin does not send any of your data to any third-party servers or services.

The Plugin does not sell, share, or disclose your data to anyone.

The Plugin uses the JDoodle Compiler API (https://www.jdoodle.com/compiler-api) to compile and run your code. The JDoodle Compiler API is a third-party service that provides online code execution for various programming languages. The JDoodle Compiler API may collect and use your code and other information in accordance with their own privacy policy (https://www.jdoodle.com/terms).

Cookies

Cookies are small files that are stored on your device by websites or Plugins that you visit. They are used to store information such as preferences and settings.

The Plugin does not use any cookies of its own. However, the JDoodle Compiler API and Google Analytics may use cookies to provide their services. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
